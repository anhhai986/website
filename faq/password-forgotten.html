Password Forgotten?

We can not help you, you need to replace password file on your own.

In short

  • replace sitecake/server/credential.php with credential.php.default

Details

Open sitecake/server folder on your hosting and find credential.php file. Delete credential.php and create new one by renaming credential.php.default to credential.php. The password will be reverted to 'admin’.

Now go to Sitecake login and click Change password link to change password to the one you can easily remember. That’s it

WARNING: Don’t be lazy and leave ‘admin’ as default password for your website.
